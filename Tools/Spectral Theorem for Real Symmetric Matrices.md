```ad-theorem
title: Spectral Theorem for Real Symmetric Matrices

Let $M$ be a real symmetric $n\times n$ matrix. Then $M$ has an orthonormal basis of eigenvectors $v_1, \ldots, v_n$ and eigenvalues $\lambda_1, \ldots, \lambda_n$ with
$$
v_iM = \lambda_iv_i
$$
for all $i$.

```
