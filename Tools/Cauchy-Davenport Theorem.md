## Theorem

```ad-theorem
title: Cauchy-Davenport Theorem

Let $p$ be a prime and let $A,B\subseteq \mathbb Z_p$. Then
$$
|A+B| \geq \min\{ |A|+|B| -1,\ p\}.
$$

```
