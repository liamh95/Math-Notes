## Line Graph

```ad-definition
title: Line Graph

Let $G = (V,E)$ be a graph. The **line graph of $G$**, $L(G)$, has $E(G)$ has its vertex set where $e,f \in E(G)$ are adjacent in $L(G)$ if and only if they are incident in $G$.

```
