```ad-theorem
title: Linear Bound for Exponential

For any real $x$,
$$
1 + x \leq e^x.
$$

```

<i>Proof.</i> The line $y= 1+x$ is tangent to $y = e^x$ at $x = 0$. Since $e^x$ is convex on all of $\mathbb R$, the graph of $e^x$ lies above this tangent line.
