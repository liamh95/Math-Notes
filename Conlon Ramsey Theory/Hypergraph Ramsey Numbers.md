```ad-definition
title: Hypergraph Ramsey Numbers

The ramsey number $r_k(s,t)$ is the smallest natural number $n$ such that, in any two-coloring of the edges of $K_n^{(k)}$, there is a red copy of $K_s^{(k)}$ or a blue copy of $K_t^{(k)}$.

```
