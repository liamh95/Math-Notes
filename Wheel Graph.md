## Definition

```ad-definition
title: Wheel Graph

A **wheel** consists of a chordless cycle $C$ called the **rim** and a vertex $v$ not in $C$ called the **center** where the center has at least three neighbors in the rim.

```


## Exceptions

Some people don't consider $K_4$ to be a wheel.
